WELCOME!
Thank you for your interest in registering to vote. We are glad to be of assistance.
Please note that the voter registration application may be used to do the following:
  • Register as a voter
  • Update your registration information.
  • Check your voter registration status
To register or update your voter registration:
Use this website to submit an application online
To submit an online application, you will need:
  • Valid Kenyan ID or Kenyan Passport
  • Current Passport photo
To be eligible to register to vote you must:
  • You must be a Kenyan citizen.
  • You must be at least 18 years of age
  • You must not have been judged "mentally incompetent" in a court of law.
  • You must not have been convicted of an election offence during the preceding five years.
